DISCLAIMER

Rooting Android will void your manufacturer's warrranty, and potentially could brick (render device unusable) your Android device. Some of these methods will not work with your device, research and see if the method is available for you device. Please root at your own risk! We are not responsible for damage to your device from rooting.

Unlocking Bootloader (Might apply to your device)

Some devices will require unlocking "bootloader" to root. Each device has different methods, please see online. To unlock bootloader on HTC, visit their HTCDev BootLoader Unlock Website.

Root: Direct root apps

Developers have made apps which root your device with an APK file you can download to your Android device and run from there. This may not work on newer firmware or on some device models. These are the ones I trust and know:

1. z4root - Official forum thread

2. Framaroot - Official forum thread

Root: Find your device

Each device has different methods, protection, and firmware. To avoid bricking, I recommend you to go on this website, and then find your device. Follow the instructions, and if it fails, you should use your favorite search engine and find more root methods appropriate for your device.
